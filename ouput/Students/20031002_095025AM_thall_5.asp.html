Please join the Campus Activities Board at the 2003 Homecoming Concert featuring the Black Eyed Peas and Gangstarr. The show will take place on Thursday, October 9 in the Towson Center Arena. Doors open at 6:30 p.m. Tickets are $15 with a Towson ID and $20 for non-students. Tickets are available in advance at the University Union Ticket Office and at all Ticketmaster locations. Remaining tickets will be sold the night of the show. For more information on this show and all C.A.B. events, please call 410-704-2070.
